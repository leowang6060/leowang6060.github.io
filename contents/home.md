#### Short Bio
Leo Wang is a research fellow at the Key Laboratory of Computational Linguistics of China’s Ministry of Education at PKU. He has published over 30 academic papers on influential journals and proceedings of international conferences and symposiums, some of which are EI indexed and have been cited frequently. He has been invited as the keynote speaker for several academic conferences as well as the reviewer of several publications, for example, proceedings of the China National Conference on Computational Linguistics and Chinese Lexical Semantic Workshop, etc. He has been the PI or Co-PI for several national and provincial research projects, such as the “National High Technology Research and Development Program of China” and the “National Key Basic Research Development Plan of China”, and so on.
